About ProVolt Electrical Services

Our Story
Local, Family-Owned in the Texas Hill Country

Founded in 2010, ProVolt Electric is a local, family-owned electrical company serving Kerrville, Fredericksburg, and the surrounding Texas Hill Country. With over 15 years in business, we provide fast, reliable electrical solutions for residential, commercial, ranch & rural, and industrial clients.

From panel replacements and new circuits to lighting installations, equipment hookups, remodeling, maintenance, and new construction, we approach every project with safety, precision, and customer satisfaction in mind. Over the years, we’ve completed work for local churches, gas stations, and research centers, earning a reputation for quality workmanship and professionalism.

Meet the Owner
Led by Master Electrician Scott Raggo

ProVolt Electric is owned and operated by Scott Raggo, a master electrician with over 15 years of hands-on experience in the electrical trade. Scott founded ProVolt on a commitment to integrity, quality, and exceptional customer service.

He leads a team of highly skilled electricians whose combined expertise spans decades. ProVolt emphasizes continuous training to stay current with the latest technology, codes, and industry standards—so whether it’s a quick repair or a major upgrade, your home or business is in experienced hands.

Every project is treated with care and respect, with clear communication from the first estimate to the final walkthrough, so you always know what to expect.
