Meet the Owner: Scott Raggo

person
Scott Raggo Owner, ProVolt Electric

Scott Raggo brings over 15 years of hands-on experience in the electrical trade. He founded ProVolt Electric on a commitment to integrity, quality, and exceptional customer service.

Scott leads a team of highly skilled electricians whose combined expertise spans decades. He encourages continuous learning to stay on top of the latest technology, equipment, and industry standards, ensuring that every project is safe, reliable, and efficient.

Whether the job is a small service call or a large commercial or industrial project, Scott remains actively involved, making sure ProVolt Electric delivers the level of service he would expect in his own home or business.
